FaQ

How does my order stay safe during transport?

Our delivery method uses 2 layers of security during order transit.
• Firstly, the bot stores your order entirely within its enderchest until it reaches your coordinates.
• Secondly, the bot flies to your coordinates to prevent most player encounters

How fast is the delivery?

Since our delivery process is completely automated, most delays are down to server lag. The transit speed of items is also very fast compared to manual shops.
